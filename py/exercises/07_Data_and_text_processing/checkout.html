Supermarket checkout

Given a file called products.txt that contains a list of the products stocked in a supermarket, with their prices, and a file called shopping.txt that contains a list of items that a shopper is purchasing, and their quantities, print the receipt, which includes a calculation of the overall cost to the shopper.

Sample output

----- Contents of file: products.txt -----
Medium Sliced White Bread - 1.40
Medium Sliced Wholemeal Bread - 1.45
Thick Sliced Granary Bread - 2.10
Baked Beans in Tomato Sauce 4 Pack - 3.99
Large Free Range Eggs 6 Pack - 1.70
Large Free Range Eggs 12 Pack - 3.30
Medium Free Range Eggs 6 Pack - 1.30
Medium Free Range Eggs 12 Pack - 2.40
Whole Milk 2 Pints - 1.25
Organic Semi Skimmed Milk 2L - 2.70
Chocolate Biscuits - 1.50
Instant Coffee - 6.00
Washing Up Liquid - 2.00

------ End of content ------

----- Contents of file: shopping.txt -----
Instant Coffee x1
Chocolate Biscuits x2
Whole Milk 2 Pints x1
Medium Sliced Wholemeal Bread x1

------ End of content ------

=== Receipt ===
Instant Coffee @ 6.00 x 1 = 6.00
Chocolate Biscuits @ 1.50 x 2 = 3.00
Whole Milk 2 Pints @ 1.25 x 1 = 1.25
Medium Sliced Wholemeal Bread @ 1.45 x 1 = 1.45

Total cost: 11.70
