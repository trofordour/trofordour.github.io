Is a number prime?

Write a program that prompts for a positive integer and then works out if the input is prime. A prime number is a whole number greater than 1 whose only factors are 1 and itself.

Sample output

Enter a positive integer and I will tell you if it is a prime number: 1

1 is not prime, by definition.


Enter a positive integer and I will tell you if it is a prime number: 2

2 is prime.


Enter a positive integer and I will tell you if it is a prime number: 3

3 is prime.


Enter a positive integer and I will tell you if it is a prime number: 4

All even numbers greater than 2 are non-prime.


Enter a positive integer and I will tell you if it is a prime number: 5

5 is prime.


Enter a positive integer and I will tell you if it is a prime number: 21

21 is not prime.


Enter a positive integer and I will tell you if it is a prime number: 23

23 is prime.
